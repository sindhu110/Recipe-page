Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tbsp of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until well mixed.
  2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Cook the omelette: Pour the eggs into the pan. Tilt the pan to spread them evenly. As the eggs start to set, gently lift the edges with a spatula, letting any uncooked egg flow underneath.
  4. Add fillings (optional): When the eggs are nearly set, add your desired fillings on one half of the omelette.
  5. Fold and serve: Carefully fold the omelette in half and slide it onto a plate. Serve hot.
